From first call to daily assistant in 48 hours

We handle all the technical setup. You just answer a few questions about your family.

1

Book a discovery call

A free 20-minute video call where we learn about your family — who's in it, what your routines look like, and what's driving you crazy. No technical knowledge needed.

2

We build your family profile

Based on our call, we set up your assistant with your family's names, schedules, meal preferences, and routines. This is done entirely by us — you don't configure anything.

3

We connect your tools

We link your Google Calendar, budget app (YNAB), grocery list (AnyList), and task manager (Notion) to your assistant. Only WhatsApp is required — everything else is optional and added at your pace.

4

Text your assistant

Open your family's WhatsApp group and say hi. Your assistant is ready to help with meals, schedules, grocery lists, budgets, reminders, and anything else your family throws at it.
