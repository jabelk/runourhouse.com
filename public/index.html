Frequently asked questions

What's included in each plan?

Every plan includes your own AI assistant on WhatsApp that knows your family. Core covers calendar and meals. Complete adds budget tracking, grocery management, and task lists. Concierge adds a monthly strategy call and priority support.

Can I switch plans?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime — changes take effect on your next billing cycle. Most families start with Complete.

What's the setup fee for?

We personally build your family profile, connect all your tools (calendar, budget, grocery list), test everything thoroughly, and do a live walkthrough with you. It's hands-on, white-glove setup — not a DIY onboarding flow.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We remove our access to your accounts, and all your data stays exactly where it is — in your own calendar, budget app, and grocery list. Nothing is lost.

Do I need all the integrations?

Nope. Only WhatsApp is required. Start with just calendar and meals, then add budget tracking or grocery management whenever you're ready. Your assistant grows with you.

Is my data safe?

Your data stays in YOUR accounts — your Google Calendar, your YNAB, your AnyList. We orchestrate between them but don't store your personal data. All communication is encrypted, and you can disconnect any integration anytime.

Do I need a WhatsApp account?

Yes — WhatsApp is how you talk to your assistant. It's free, works on any smartphone, and most families already have it. If you don't, it takes about 2 minutes to set up.

How quickly can I get started?

Book a discovery call today, and your assistant can be live within 48 hours. Most of the setup happens on our end — you just answer a few questions about your family and routines.

Built by a parent, for parents

Run Our House started in one family's kitchen in Reno, Nevada. Jason, a software engineer and dad of two young girls, built an AI assistant for his own family after watching his wife Erin juggle calendars, meal planning, grocery lists, and budgets across five different apps — while also keeping track of everything in her head.

What started as a weekend project became something their family couldn't live without. Friends started asking "can I get one of those?" — and Run Our House was born. Every feature exists because a real family needed it, not because a product team hypothesized about it.

Run Our House is operated by Sierra Code Co, a software consultancy based in Reno, NV. Jason personally deploys and manages every family's assistant — this isn't a faceless SaaS product. When you have a question, you talk to the person who built it.

Your data stays in YOUR accounts

Your calendar events stay in Google Calendar. Your budget stays in YNAB. Your grocery list stays in AnyList. We orchestrate between them — we don't store copies of your personal data.
